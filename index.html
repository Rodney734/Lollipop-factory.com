About Us

Welcome to The Lollipop Factory

Located in the heart of Lilongwe, The Lollipop Factory is a mobile beverage business dedicated to crafting high-quality drinks for all events. We specialize in creating exquisite cocktails and mocktails that delight the senses and elevate your drinking experience.

Our skilled mixologists combine premium spirits, fresh ingredients, and innovative techniques to create beverages that are as visually stunning as they are delicious.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, hosting a corporate event, or simply enjoying a night out, our creative concoctions will transform your experience into something truly memorable.
